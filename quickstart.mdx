<Callout kind="info" title="Prerequisites">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A valid email address for verification
- Basic information about your role (Model, Business, Studio, or Enterprise)
- Profile photo and portfolio details ready (optional for initial setup)
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up for ModelBoard in under 2 minutes. The process verifies your email and guides you through account selection.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to [https://modelboard.net/signup](https://modelboard.net/signup).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a password, and accept the terms of service.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification link and click to confirm.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Answer initial questions about your industry role to proceed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Initial Profile Setup

After selecting your type, complete your profile to unlock full features.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Profile Photo" icon="image">
    Add a professional headshot from your device.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill Basic Info" icon="edit-3">
    Enter your name, location, and industry experience.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Build Portfolio" icon="folder">
    Upload samples, links, or credits (skip for now if not ready).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Preferences" icon="settings">
    Configure notifications and privacy settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
