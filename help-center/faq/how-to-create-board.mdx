## Overview

To organize your docs, start by creating a new board.

## Steps

1. Log in to your ModelBoard account.
2. Click "New Board" in the sidebar.
3. Enter a board name (e.g. "API Docs").
4. (Optional) Add a description.
5. Click "Create" to finish.

Your new board is ready for pages and docs!
